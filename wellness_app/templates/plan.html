✨ Wellness Tips

Daily Habits That Compound

🌅

Morning Ritual

Wake up at the same time daily. 10 minutes of sunlight exposure within 30 mins of waking regulates your circadian rhythm and boosts energy all day.

😴

Sleep is Training

80% of muscle recovery happens during sleep. Aim for 7–9 hours. Avoid screens 1 hour before bed and keep your room cool and dark.

🧠

Mindset Matters

Track your progress weekly, not daily. Motivation fluctuates — discipline doesn't. Celebrate small wins every single week.

📵

Stress Management

High cortisol (stress hormone) directly blocks fat loss and muscle gain. 10 minutes of daily meditation or deep breathing makes a measurable difference.
